Introduction

The Semiconductor Skills Bridge Academy (SSBA) internship at Ohlone College provided hands-on training in semiconductor manufacturing, robotics, and cleanroom protocols. This program was designed to equip students with the skills needed for careers in the semiconductor industry.

  • Cleanroom protocols
  • Career preparation

The internship offered practical experience and collaborative learning, preparing students for future roles in engineering and technology.

Lam Research

Lam Research is a leading provider of wafer fabrication equipment and services for the global semiconductor industry. Specializing in etch, deposition, and clean technologies, their tools are essential for producing integrated circuits, memory chips, and other electronic components. Lam drives innovation, enabling smaller, faster, and more efficient devices.

Integrated Technical Solutions (ITS)

Integrated Technical Solutions (ITS) delivers advanced services for semiconductor manufacturing, focusing on equipment maintenance, process optimization, and technical support. Their expertise ensures efficient, reliable operations in production facilities.

Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM)

An SEM (Scanning Electron Microscope) uses a focused beam of electrons to produce highly detailed images of a sample's surface, revealing fine structures at high magnification.
