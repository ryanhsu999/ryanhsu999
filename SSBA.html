Lam Research

Lam Research is a leading provider of wafer fabrication equipment and services for the global semiconductor industry. Specializing in etch, deposition, and clean technologies, their tools are essential for producing integrated circuits, memory chips, and other electronic components. Known for innovation, Lam plays a key role in advancing semiconductor technology, enabling smaller, faster, and more efficient devices.

Integrated Technical Solutions (ITS)

Integrated Technical Solutions (ITS) provides advanced services for semiconductor manufacturing, specializing in equipment maintenance, process optimization, and technical support. Their work ensures efficient, reliable operations in production facilities, supporting the industry's overall success.

Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM)

An SEM (Scanning Electron Microscope) uses a focused beam of electrons to produce highly detailed images of a sample's surface.
