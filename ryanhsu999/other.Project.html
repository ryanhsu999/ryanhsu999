Ohlone College course: ETHN-100-01: Introduction to Ethnic Studies.

Banned Book Project: Ban Voyage Volume 1 (Fall 2024) - Jay Chou Struggle Studies
9/27/2024 to 12/12/2024

Each student in the course had the unique opportunity to develop a children's story that might face challenges or even bans in schools and libraries due to its themes.

I am deeply grateful to the Ethnic Studies department, and The Lytton Center for the Public Good for fund the project.

Lastly I would like to thank Ohlone college Faculty and staff: Dr. August Samie, and Public Services Librarian: Nnekay FitzClarke for their generous support in bringing this project to life.

A copy of everyone’s stories is proudly available for public viewing at the Ohlone Fremont Library
